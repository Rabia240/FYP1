Schedule Automation

Automate your schedule with ease

Scheduling Builder is an automated schedule management system. Scheduling is a challenge that almost educational insituitions face. Creating and managing a manual schedule is a very difficult task for administrator of educational insituitions.

Teacher Dashboard

Teacher can view the student schedule and arrange extra class. Teachers will get notification if no room is empty for extra class.

Student Dashboard

Students can view schedule and if the schedule will update,then we will get a notification regarding any change in schedule.

Mobile App

The student login to the mobile application using their credentials.. The credentials are verified against a database of authorized student.
